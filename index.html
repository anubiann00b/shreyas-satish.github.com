I'm Shreyas Satish and an engineer at Mapunity. I graduated in 2010 from BNMIT (VTU), Bangalore, India with an engineering degree in Information Science.

At Mapunity, I'm currently developing Technagara, a City Management/Information System, a Bangalore instance of which, is bcity. Technagara aims to engage civil socities in governance, by giving them a platform to report and discuss issues, access Governance based news articles, track ongoing projects in cities and download Govenment forms.
This app uses a stack of PostgreSQL, Ruby, Rails, jQuery, OpenLayers and HTML5.

I love and use OpenSource software. I'm currently developing WorldView, a wrapper/extension to OpenLayers to make working with OpenLayers, hence maps, trivial.

When not geeking out, I'm usually following football (soccer). I'm a Chelsea F.C fan.
